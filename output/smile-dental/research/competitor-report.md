## Cross-Competitor Patterns

### Table Stakes (what EVERYONE does — we MUST have)
1. **Pricing tables** with specific VND amounts by implant brand (Korean, Swiss, American tiers)
2. **Before/after patient photos** with real names
3. **Doctor credentials** prominently displayed (degrees, experience years)
4. **Payment plan / trả góp options** highlighted
5. **All-on-4 and All-on-6** as separate sections for toàn hàm patients

### Differentiators (what only 1-2 do — potential advantage)
1. **Video testimonials** from real patients (ages, cities named) — only Dr. Care does this well
2. **Question-format headline** matching search intent — only Dr. Care does this
3. **Implant-only specialization** as positioning — only Dr. Care claims this
4. **Press/media mentions** as trust signals — I-Dent and Dr. Care

### Gaps (what NOBODY does — our biggest opportunity)
1. **No above-fold CTA on any page** — all 3 require scrolling to find the conversion action. Smile Dental can win with CTA visible immediately.
2. **No pain/fear addressal above fold** — "có đau không?" is the #1 patient concern but NO competitor addresses it in the hero. We should lead with "Không Đau" promise.
3. **No clear guarantee** — nobody offers an explicit satisfaction guarantee or warranty prominently. A "Bảo hành trọn đời" or "Cam kết không đau" in the hero would differentiate.
4. **All have navigation menus** — every competitor leaks traffic through nav links. Our LP will be distraction-free.
5. **No urgency with real deadline** — I-Dent has monthly promos but no countdown or hard limit. A specific deadline creates action.
