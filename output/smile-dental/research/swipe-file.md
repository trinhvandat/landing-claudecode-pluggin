## Key Takeaways for Our Page

1. **Message**: Lead with the patient's question ("Giá bao nhiêu?" or "Có đau không?"), not our brand name
2. **Structure**: CTA above fold (gap!), pricing early, trust before asking for conversion
3. **Trust**: Video testimonials are the gold standard — Dr. Care proves this works for 50+ demo
4. **CTA**: Personal language ("cho tôi"), low commitment ("tư vấn miễn phí"), 2 fields max
5. **Design**: Warm CTA color against professional medical palette, no navigation, no distractions
